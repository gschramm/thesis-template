The \ac{cdf} is given by~\cite{johnson2022, wilson2023}:
\begin{equation}
  F(x) = \int_{-\infty}^{x} \frac{1}{\sigma\sqrt{2\pi}}\,
         \exp\!\left(-\frac{(t-\mu)^2}{2\sigma^2}\right) dt,
\end{equation}
where $\mu$ is the mean and $\sigma^2$ the variance.
