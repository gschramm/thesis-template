\section{Some math}

The convolution of two functions $f$ and $g$ is defined as
\begin{equation}
  (f * g)(t) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f(\tau)\, g(t - \tau)\, d\tau,
\end{equation}
which appears frequently in signal processing and probability theory.
The \ac{snr} of the recovered signal depends directly on the chosen kernel.
